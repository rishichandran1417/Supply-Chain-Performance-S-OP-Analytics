# 🔄 Supply Chain Performance & S&OP Analytics

An end-to-end supply chain analytics solution focused on supplier performance, service-level monitoring, forecast accuracy measurement, and procurement analytics. Built using Microsoft Excel to support Sales & Operations Planning (S&OP) through KPI-driven decision making.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

| Attribute       | Detail          |
| --------------- | --------------- |
| Suppliers       | 5               |
| SKUs            | 8               |
| Regions         | 4               |
| Purchase Orders | 640             |
| Analysis Period | Jul–Dec 2024    |
| Tools           | Microsoft Excel |

---

## 🔍 Key Modules

### 🚚 Supplier Performance Analysis

* Benchmarked 5 suppliers using OTIF, Fill Rate, and Lead Time Variance metrics.
* Identified **SUP005** as the lowest-performing supplier with an OTIF of **27.4%** compared to a network average above 30%.
* Evaluated supplier reliability using delivery commitment adherence and service-level performance.

---

### 📈 Forecast Performance Monitoring

* Measured demand planning effectiveness using Forecast Accuracy metrics.
* Achieved overall Forecast Accuracy of **90.91%** across the analysis period.
* Compared planning performance against industry best-practice benchmarks.

---

### 📦 Inventory & Service-Level Analytics

* Calculated Fill Rate and order fulfillment KPIs across 640 purchase orders.
* Achieved an overall Fill Rate of **93.63%**.
* Identified fulfillment gaps and potential service-level risks affecting customer satisfaction.

---

### ⏱ Lead Time Variance Analysis

* Compared promised delivery dates against actual supplier delivery performance.
* Calculated an average Lead Time Variance of **+0.51 days**.
* Highlighted supplier-specific variability impacting planning stability.

## 💡 Key Insights

### 1. Supplier Reliability Remains a Challenge

OTIF performance was only **32.4%**, indicating significant opportunities to improve delivery consistency and service levels.

### 2. Forecasting Process Is Relatively Mature

Forecast Accuracy reached **90.91%**, demonstrating strong planning discipline despite supplier performance issues.

### 3. Fill Rate Performance Remains Strong

The network achieved a Fill Rate of **93.63%**, maintaining customer service levels despite delivery variability.

### 4. SUP005 Represents the Highest Operational Risk

SUP005 recorded the lowest OTIF performance and should be prioritized for supplier improvement initiatives.

### 5. Lead Time Variability Impacts Planning Stability

Average lead time variance of **0.51 days** introduces uncertainty into procurement and inventory planning decisions.
